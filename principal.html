Founder


“Education is the key to success, It is an ongoing process.Each day of existence, Adds to our knowledge, wisdom and experience.”


It gives me immense pleasure to announce that our college Ramji lal Inter College is progressing by leaps and bounds. We are committed to make special efforts to lay a strong foundation for learning so that our students can build their great future. I deeply and truly extent my heart-felt wishes to hon’able Founder Manager Shri Ashok Lawaniya who has always been a source of inspiration and motivation for us. He reminds us of a popular saying ‘ Where there is a will there is a way……’ following his principles we have inculcated such ethical values and habits which will help in development of their over-all personality and their career.

We are preparing our students to be good citizens of our country and participate in nation building through whatever work they would be engaged in their future career Our school is a community of learners in which all children are encouraged to achieve academic and social success, to enjoy learning, to celebrate diversity, to develop analytical skills, and to model exemplary behavior. We empower our students to take responsibility to face challenges in life. They are active participants, and every child has a responsibility not just to perform a task, but to do it to the best of her or his ability.

Our staff works together to create a nurturing environment so that students can take risks without anxiety, can think critically, and are encouraged to ask questions freely…. The curriculum, components with all traditional subject areas emphasizes high academic standards and high expectations. Teachers incorporate a variety of instructional strategies to address individual learning styles. Parents are encouraged to actively participate in school activities directly related to their Child’s educational experience and academic success in school.

– Mr. Kuldeep Kr. Verma
